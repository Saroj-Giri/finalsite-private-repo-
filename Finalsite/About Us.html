How WE Came To Be?

Our story began at CERN, where the web was born and where our founding team met. In 2010, on the 21th anniversary of the web, we built TunnelMail to make online privacy a reality again for millions of people around the world. The TunnelVPN project was born out of a need to better protect the activists and journalists that use TunnelMail.

In the years since we began as a crowdfunded project, TunnelMail and TunnelVPN continue to be primarily supported by our community and developed with community input. When it comes to fighting for online security and privacy, we have a long track record. Whether it is challenging governments, educating the public, or training journalists, we're committed to staying on the front lines.
